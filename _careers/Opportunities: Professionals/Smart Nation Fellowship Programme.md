<h4>Make an impact with your expertise</h4>
<p>Singapore boldly dreams of becoming a Smart Nation, one that harnesses
technology and data to improve the lives of citizens. To make this dream
a reality, data scientists, engineers, software developers, technologists,
designers and applied researchers must band together, share their expertise
and co-create.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Are you an expert in one of the following domains?</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Artificial Intelligence (AI)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Blockchain</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cloud computing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Data Science</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>App development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cybersecurity</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Internet of Things</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>If so, step up to the challenge of engineering new solutions at GovTech
by joining our Smart Nation Fellowship Programme!&nbsp;</p>
<p>For a span of 3 to 12 months, you will get to work on projects that we
have identified or even propose new innovative ones. A part-time consultancy
can be arranged according to your schedule.</p>
