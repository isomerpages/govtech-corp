a need for skilled talents who dare to dream big and desire to co-create
a world of digital possibilities. The Smart Nation Scholarship, jointly
offered by GovTech, Cyber Security Agency of Singapore and Infocomm Media
Development Authority, aims to give promising individuals like you the
opportunity to jump-start a dynamic career in tech. During your course
of study, you'll learn how innovation can be harnessed to serve the public
good.</p>

<h3>Benefits of being a Smart Nation Scholar</h3>
<h4>1. Employer-sponsored training</h4>
<p>As a GovTech scholar, you will receive training funds to attend professional
development courses.</p>
<h4>2. Direct entry to TAP</h4>
<p>Hone your leadership and technical abilities through GovTech's Technology
Associate Programme (TAP).</p>
<h4>3. Get access to mentors</h4>
<p>Raise all your questions about a career in tech during the regular check-ins
with your seniors.</p>
<h4>4. Expand your network</h4>
<p>Make meaningful connections with our engineers, leaders and alumni.</p>
<h4>5. Exposure to different sectors</h4>
<p>With the chance to rotate among different technology tracks, you will
be able to find your true interest.</p>
<h4>6. Demonstrate your leadership</h4>
<p>Become a leader that operates by the GovTech way: Act with Purpose, Model
Empathy and Develop People.</p>
