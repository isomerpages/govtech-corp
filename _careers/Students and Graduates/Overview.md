<h3>Have a hand in shaping Singapore's Smart Nation plans</h3>
<p>Kickstart a lifelong career with GovTech</p>
<p>Due to its ever-changing nature, the tech industry offers you a vibrant
career trajectory, one that is replete with learning and growth opportunities.
Hoping to become a professional in the tech industry? Get the head start
you need by joining one of GovTech's programmes! Ranked among Singapore's
top 10 leading graduate employers in IT and Technology according to a 2023
survey by <a href="https://gradsingapore.com/singapore100/sector/it-and-technology" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">gradsingapore</a>,
GovTech offers an array of programmes — from a technology boot camp to
internships and scholarships – to help you achieve your unique career aspirations!</p>
