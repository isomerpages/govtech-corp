<h3>Hiring Right</h3>
<p>Our recipe for success at GovTech is hiring RIGHT! We need people who
share the same excitement and passion – individuals who are excited about
building the Government Tech Stack, and developing meaningful products,
platforms, and solutions for the public good.</p>
<p>We win by keeping our hiring bar high. By hiring the best people, we can
scale up our Smart Nation initiatives and drive public sector digital transformation
as fast as we can. We are seeking talented individuals to build our capabilities
in application development, data science &amp; artificial intelligence,
cybersecurity, digital infrastructure, and sensors &amp; IoT.</p>
<p>Because hiring well is important at GovTech, we want you to understand
the nuts and bolts of our hiring process, so that you are prepared in your
career journey. We don’t make the mistake of missing out hiring that great
person.</p>
<h3>Our Hiring Process</h3>
<h5>Apply</h5>
<p>Paper applications are passé. Electronic applications save paper and reduce
our carbon footprint at the same time.</p>
<p>If there’s an available role here that excites you, one that puts your
skills to good use and lets you grow professionally, apply for it! (Electronically
of course.)</p>
<p><strong>Focus on your CV</strong> Make it easy to read:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Select a clean format which is logical and easy to read</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Make good use of bold and italic typefaces to highlight accomplishments</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Put across accomplishments, not job descriptions</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Share what you did in your role, not what the role was about</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Quantify and qualify your accomplishments - List accomplishments unique
to you, not a list of what someone else or your contribution to a team.
Highlight the benefits incurred as a result of what you did
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Include all the relevant certifications in your CV
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5>Interview</h5>
<p><strong>Phone Interview</strong>
</p>
<p>Our recruitment team will conduct the first round of interviews by phone.
The recruiter in- charge of the role will give you a call to discuss your
relevant experience, skills, career aspirations, and share more information
about the role.</p>
<p>This is also your perfect chance to learn more about GovTech and the role
in question!</p>
<p><strong>Face-to-face interview</strong>
</p>
<p>If you’re shortlisted after the phone interview, we’ll reach out and arrange
a face-to-face interview. During these interviews, you’ll get to meet our
recruiters, hiring managers, and possibly your potential teammates. These
interviews generally span 45 minutes to 1 hour. Depending on the role,
you may be required to complete technical assessments or case studies and
further rounds of interviews.</p>
<p><strong>What will be discussed during the interview?</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Role-related questions</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Technical questions related to the role will be asked for an assessment
of your technical competencies</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Share with us your previous experiences that will directly contribute
to your success in not just the role you’re interviewing for, but also
in meeting GovTech’s wider goals</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Thinking and analytical skills
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>We are not looking for model answers. We want to see how you break down
challenges, and the thought process that led to your solution</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="3" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Fit with GovTech’s A-B-C values
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Share how you live out our values of being Agile, Bold, and Collaborative
in your personal and professional life</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="4" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Questions
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Interviews are a 2-way process. We’ve put you in front of a panel of GovTechies
for you to ask all the questions you need</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>How to prepare for the interview?</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Do your homework. Knowledge about GovTech and understanding the job you
are applying could give you the advantage
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Anticipate questions and answer each of them with a story that will allow
you to put your point across
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Substantiate your answers with figures to give the panel a better understanding
of your achievements
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Showcase your critical thinking skills by suggesting ways in which you
will revisit certain solutions and better them</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="2">
<li>
<p>Grab this opportunity to inquire deeper about the role or about us</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Make a list of questions that you’d like us to answer.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>We might not have all the answers during the interview, but we’ll definitely
look for the answers and get back to you</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5>Decision</h5>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>We will objectively review your application after all the interview rounds,
before making our decision based on trusted frameworks and pre-determined
criteria</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you’re selected for the position, we will contact you after the final
interview with a verbal offer</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Thereafter, you can expect to start your adventure as a GovTechie.</p>
</li>
</ol>
