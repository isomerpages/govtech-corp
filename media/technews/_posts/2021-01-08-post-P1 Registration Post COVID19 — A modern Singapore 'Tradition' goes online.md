<p>In the interest of public health and safety, MOE moved the 2020 P1 Registration

<h3>Developed with an agile mindset!</h3>
<p>For years, MOE had considered making the P1 registration process online.

<p>Instead of waiting for the full product to be built, they launched it

<p>In the words of Senior Project Manager Tan Yeo Kwang, agile was one of

<p>‚ÄúThe solution does not have to be perfect at the initial launch. It‚Äôs
