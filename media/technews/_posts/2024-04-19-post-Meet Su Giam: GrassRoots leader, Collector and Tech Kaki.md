<h3>What initially inspired you to join Tech Kaki?</h3>
<p>The first time I knew about Tech Kaki was when I participated in <a href="/products-and-services/for-citizens/crowdsourcing/crowdtasksg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>CrowdTaskSG surveys</u></a>;

<p>Later in December 2022, I was asked by a team from GovTech to help with

<p>The purpose of the usability test was to gauge my first-hand experience

<p>The experience, along with seeing GovTech‚Äôs approach to developing technology

<h3>What makes you want to contribute to the community?</h3>
<p>Joining the Tech Kaki community gives me a chance to share my interest

<p>At the same time, I enjoy keeping up with the latest technology and app

seniors to provide feedback on apps like LifeSG and explore the use of

<h3>Have you encountered any challenges while working on projects, and how did you overcome them?</h3>
<p>In 2020, I helped start the ‚ÄúSenior Go Digital‚Äù program, where we faced
challenges in teaching seniors to use new technology via digital ambassadors.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The idea was to provide one-to-one guidance on how to use social media,
such as WhatsApp, Facebook, Telegram, Zoom sessions, etc., as well as make
e-payments via their smartphones, e.g., EPLJ (E-Payment Learning Journey).</p>
<p>Outside of the expected challenges of vision and hearing, I also noticed
a significant language barrier! The majority of our presentation slides,
pamphlets and posters were in English; most of the seniors were more familiar
with Mandarin.</p>
<p>To overcome this, I took the initiative to translate these materials into
Mandarin so that the seniors could better understand them.</p>
<p>Before the usability test for SupportGoWhere, my prior knowledge of Government
Technology (GT) was quite limited. However, this experience ignited a keen
interest in understanding how I could actively contribute to shaping Singapore‚Äôs
digital landscape; thus, I joined Tech Kaki.</p>
<p>As a grassroots leader, my primary concern has always been the welfare

representation in the development of digital products so that no one within
the community is left behind. This sense of responsibility and purpose
is what drives me to continue contributing. Whether it‚Äôs making a significant
impact or simply playing a small part, the knowledge that I am making a
