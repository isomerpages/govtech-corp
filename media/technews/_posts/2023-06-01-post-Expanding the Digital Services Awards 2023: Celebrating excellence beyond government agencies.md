<div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Digital Services Awards 2023 nominations open" src="/images/technews/Expanding_the_Digital_Services_Awards_2023__celebrating_excellence_beyond_government_agencies.jpg"></div><p>The Digital Services Awards, an annual event dedicated to recognising exceptional digital service standards, will be expanding beyond government agencies to feature categories solely for the industry sector, encouraging broader participation and acknowledging the valuable contributions of citizens and private organisations.</p><p>This year will be the Awards‚Äô third edition and see the introduction of a new award category, ‚ÄúOutstanding Citizen Contributor‚Äù, which will recognise citizens who have actively participated in and contributed to improving government digital services. Additionally, the categories of ‚ÄúBest Usability‚Äù and ‚ÄúMost Popular Informational Website‚Äù will be open to industry nominations for the first time, with a public and private sector winner for each category.</p><p>While a significant portion of the awards focuses on technical excellence, powered by the Whole-of-government Application Analytics (WOGAA), other categories acknowledge user experience, visual appeal, and qualitative aspects. This balanced approach celebrates holistic achievements.</p><p>Further details of each award category are available at the end of this article.</p><h3>Industry award nominations now open, government award nominations in June</h3><p>Nominations for the Best Usability and Most Popular Informational Website (Industry) awards are now open. If you have a website that meets the eligibility criteria below, seize this opportunity to showcase your achievements and be recognised for your efforts.</p><p>Eligibility criteria for Best Usability and Most Popular Informational Website awards include:</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p><strong>Singapore Domain:</strong> Only websites with .sg domains (including redirected sites) are eligible.</p></li><li><p><strong>Public Access: </strong>The website must be fully accessible to the public.</p></li><li><p><strong>Informational:</strong> The primary function of the website should be to provide information, with no more than 20% of transactional pages. E-commerce, social media, service/event/job/gaming portals do not qualify.</p></li><li><p><strong>Non-government:</strong> The website should not have a <a href="https://go.gov.sg/dsa2023nominations-technews" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">gov.sg</a> domain.</p></li><li><p><strong>Minimum Web Traffic: </strong>The website must have a minimum of 3,000 visits/sessions per month.</p></li><li><p><strong>Safety: </strong>Obscene, illegal, or socially harmful websites will not be entertained.</p></li></ul><p>To submit your nominations, visit the&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/dsa2023nominations-technews" class="editor-rtfLink" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Digital Services Awards website</a>&nbsp;and follow the instructions provided. The deadline for nominations is July 31, 2023, 6 pm.</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Evaluation process timeline for the Digital Services Awards 2023" src="/images/technews/Expanding_the_Digital_Services_Awards_2023__celebrating_excellence_beyond_government_agencies__2_.jpg"></div><p><em>Important dates to take note of!</em></p><p>As for government agencies, the government nomination form will be sent via GovTech DigitalGov by the first half of June.</p><p>To ensure fairness and appropriate representation, government agencies will be grouped based on their digital maturity and resources owned, resulting in three size classifications: Large, Medium, and Small, determined by the number of Informational Websites owned.</p><h3>Evaluation timeline and criteria</h3><p>The evaluation process consists of three tiers, with evaluation conducted by both experts and citizen judges:</p><h4>Tier 1: Establishing the Top 10 - Pre-screening Evaluation (Aug-Sep 2023)</h4><p>After the nomination period ends, the nominated Informational Websites will undergo pre-screening based on GovTech‚Äôs&nbsp;<a href="https://www.tech.gov.sg/files/digital-transformation/DSS%20for%20Public%202020.pdf" class="editor-rtfLink" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Digital Services Standards.</a>&nbsp;The top 10 nominees fulfilling the baseline standards of intuitivity, usability, accessibility, and inclusivity will proceed to the next round and automatically become nominees for the Most Popular Informational Website award.</p><p>The top 10 nominees will be revealed in September 2023 on the Digital Services Awards website.</p><h4>Tier 2: Establishing the Top 3 - Judges Evaluation (Sep-Oct 2023)</h4><p>A panel of judges will evaluate the top 10 nominees based on Nielsen Norman‚Äôs Group 10 Usability Heuristics for&nbsp;<a href="https://www.nngroup.com/articles/ten-usability-heuristics/" class="editor-rtfLink" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">User Interface Design</a>,&nbsp;<a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/" class="editor-rtfLink" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1</a>, and their own expert domain knowledge. The top three nominees with the best scores will advance to the final round.</p><h4>Tier 3: Establishing the Final Winner - Citizen Evaluation (Oct-Nov 2023)</h4><p>One hundred citizen evaluators, including Persons with Disabilities (PWDs), will assess the top three nominees using the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.nngroup.com/videos/system-usability-scale/" class="editor-rtfLink" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">System Usability Scale (SUS)</a>. The nominee with the highest combined score from the judges and citizen evaluation will win.</p><h3>Take part in raising digital standards in Singapore</h3><p>With the expanded award categories and the opportunity for industry nominations, the Digital Services Awards 2023 hopes to spur individuals and businesses to enhance the usability and inclusiveness of local digital services.</p><p>For more information and to submit your nominations, visit the Digital Services Awards&nbsp;<a href="http://go.gov.sg/dsa2023-technews" class="editor-rtfLink" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">website</a>.
