<h3>1. Punggol Digital District</h3>
<p>Set to be launched progressively from 2024, the <a href="/media/technews/building-an-operating-system-for-punggol-digital-district/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Punggol Digital District</u></a> will

<p>For starters, facial recognition technology will be used for everything

<p>The new Singapore Institute of Technology‚Äôs campus will also be located
