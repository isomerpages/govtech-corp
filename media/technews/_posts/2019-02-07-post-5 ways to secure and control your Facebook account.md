<h3>AI @ Facebook</h3>
<p>Given the massive number of Facebook users and their interactions on the

<p>This is where AI is being used to monitor and flag violations to community

<p>‚ÄúTo give you an example, in the third quarter of 2018, we removed three

<p>Winding down the discussion, Dr Andrade emphasised that AI development
