<p>TechHunt, our exclusive recruitment event series, is back in person! We
are hiring technology enthusiasts with the same excitement and passion
as us to build tech for the public good.</p>
<p>This year, we are calling for tech talents in the roles of Application
Infrastructure Engineer/ Infrastructure Engineer, Digital Business Analyst,
Project Manager (Agile) and Software Engineer to join our growing team!</p>
<p>This would be a great opportunity to network with our tech leads, recruiters,
and like-minded peers from the industry. Here’s the best part – successful
candidates will be notified of their application status within 48 hours
of the event. Food and drinks are provided!</p>
