<p>Join Singapore’s first AI Security focused CTF, featuring machine learning
security challenges in 7 domains!</p>
<p>Here’s what you can look forward to:</p>
<h3>Event Details</h3>
<p>The AI Security CTF will be held in two rounds – a virtual preliminary
round, followed by 4-hour finals.</p>
<p></p>
<h4>Round 1: 12-Hour Preliminary Virtual Round</h4>
<p>Start Time: Saturday, 26<sup>th</sup> Oct 6pm (UTC+08 Singapore Time)</p>
<p>End Time: Sunday, 27<sup>th</sup> October 6am (UTC+08 Singapore Time)</p>
<p><strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/singaporeaictf" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">REGISTER NOW</a></strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<h4>Round 2: 4-Hour Finals (Local teams to attend physically at MBS)</h4>
<p>Start Time: Tuesday, 5<sup>th</sup> Nov 1:30pm (UTC+08 Singapore Time)</p>
<p>End Time: Tuesday, 5<sup>th</sup> Nov 5:30pm (UTC+08 Singapore Time)</p>
<p></p>
<h3>Schedule</h3>
<p>All dates and times are provided in UTC+08 and are subject to change.</p>
