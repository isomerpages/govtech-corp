<h3>About GovTech ReCONNECT</h3>
<p>Once a part of GovTech, always a part of GovTech. This community was set
up in 2019 for GovTech alumni to stay abreast of tech developments in the
government and continue to support the Smart Nation initiative in their
own ways.</p>
<p>We have over 600 members who get opportunities for networking, knowledge
sharing and referral programmes.</p>
<p>Read on to learn about the perks of signing up for GovTech ReCONNECT.</p>
