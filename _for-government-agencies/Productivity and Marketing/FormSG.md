<h3>Build secure government forms in minutes with FormSG</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Products and Services/FormSG.jpg">
</div>
<p>FormSG is a form builder tool that enables public officers to create digital
government forms in minutes, replacing the use of paper forms.</p>
<p>Collect data from residents and businesses securely.</p>
<p>Over 600,000 forms were launched across 160 government agencies.</p>
<p>To showcase more impact, show examples of the critical forms that FormSG
powered during Covid - <a href="https://form.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://form.gov.sg/</a> -&gt; Supporting-national-and-emergent-use-cases.</p>
<h3>Why use FormSG?</h3>
<p>Drag and drop builder allows you to launch forms in minutes, with over
20 types of fields to choose from.</p>
<p>Speed up form filling with pre-filled data from Myinfo.</p>
<p>All form responses are either encrypted end-to-end or sent directly to
email inbox. Third parties, including FormSG, will not be able to access
or view your form data.</p>
<h3>How to use FormSG?</h3>
<p>Simply log in with a .<a href="http://gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">gov.sg</a>, a whitelisted email address, or via Singpass
on the <a href="https://form.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">FormSG website</a> to
get started.</p>
