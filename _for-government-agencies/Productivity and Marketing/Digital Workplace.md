<h3>Creating a seamless and collaborative working environment for public officers with Digital Workplace</h3>
<p>Housing a suite of productivity tools and digital enablers, the Digital
Workplace is designed to allow public officers of any division and tech
literacy to move through the workday with ease. The suite comprises synced
calendars, biometric logins, visitor management systems and more.</p>

<h3>Creating a seamless and collaborative working environment for public officers with Digital Workplace (DWP)</h3>
<p>The Digital Workplace comprises a suite of productivity tools and digital
enablers, designed to allow public officers of any division and tech literacy
to move through the workday with ease, tapping on the power of today's
digital tools.</p>
<p>This ranges from synced calendars, biometric logins, visitor management
systems and more.</p>
