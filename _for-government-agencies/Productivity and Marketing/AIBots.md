<hr>
<p><a href="https://aibots.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>AIBots</u></a> is
a platform where Singapore Public Officers can create, customise, and deploy
Generative AI chatbots quickly and efficiently. By leveraging Retrieval-augmented
Generation (RAG), AIBots enhances chatbot responses by integrating internal
agency knowledge bases, bridging the gap between publicly available information
and internal resources.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Since its launch, AIBots has seen rapid adoption, with 40,000 users across
115 agencies, 12,000 bots created, and over 1 million messages sent up
to Feb 2025.</p>
<h3>Why use AIBots? &nbsp;</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="AIBots dashboard" src="/images/Products and Services/For Government Agencies/AIBots_dashboard.png">
</div>
<p>AIBots allows public service officers to create and deploy customised
AI chatbots tailored to their specific needs. Here's how:&nbsp;</p>
<div class="isomer-card-grid">
<div class="isomer-card">
<div class="isomer-card-body">
<div class="isomer-card-title">Upload internal documents</div>
<div class="isomer-card-description">Incorporate content classified up to Restricted / Sensitive (Normal),
allowing the bot to reference information that is not available on the
internet and unique to your agency.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="isomer-card">
<div class="isomer-card-body">
<div class="isomer-card-title">Configure your bot</div>
<div class="isomer-card-description">Engineer system prompts for the bot to respond exactly the way you want.
Set the tone, outline guardrails, and try different creativity levels to
make your bot suit your specific use case. </div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="isomer-card">
<div class="isomer-card-body">
<div class="isomer-card-title">Share with others</div>
<div class="isomer-card-description">Share your customised bot with team members, multiplying the benefits
of what you have built across your organisation.</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<h4>Use Cases: Examples of how public officers have leveraged AIBots&nbsp;</h4>
<div class="isomer-card-grid">
<div class="isomer-card">
<div class="isomer-card-body">
<div class="isomer-card-title">Talking points and speech generator</div>
<div class="isomer-card-description">Upload relevant documents and past speeches to generate tailored talking
points or speech drafts in your desired style, length, and context.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="isomer-card">
<div class="isomer-card-body">
<div class="isomer-card-title">Q&amp;A on internal processes</div>
<div class="isomer-card-description">Create a Q&amp;A chatbot to handle frequent inquiries about internal processes

with just the original policy document uploaded.</div>
