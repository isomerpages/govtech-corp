<hr>
<p><strong>SearchSG</strong> is an AI-powered search engine designed to improve
access to government-related information. Tailored to the Singapore context,
it enhances search relevance, accuracy, and user satisfaction, helping
citizens quickly and efficiently find accurate information across all government
websites. Since its beta launch in June 2022, SearchSG has facilitated
over 3.73 million searches, averaging 300,000 searches per month. The platform
has enabled government agencies to enhance content and user experience
based on real-time search data. Additionally, SearchSG has reduced search
time by 67%, saving users an average of 6.1 minutes per search.</p>

<h3>How to Use SearchSG?</h3>
<p><strong>For Citizens:</strong>
<br>SearchSG is integrated into several government websites such as <a href="https://mom.gov.sg/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">MOM</a>, <a href="https://www.mfa.gov.sg/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">MFA</a>, <a href="https://www.edb.gov.sg/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">EDB</a>, and <a href="http://iras.gov.sg/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">IRAS</a>. You can try
out the search engine on these sites, with plans for further integration
across additional government platforms in the future. For more information,
visit the <a href="https://www.search.gov.sg/citizens" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">SearchSG website</a>.</p>
<p><strong>For Government Agencies:</strong>
<br>If your agency is interested in onboarding SearchSG, please <a href="https://go.gov.sg/ssgcontact-gtweb" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">contact us </a>or
visit our <a href="https://www.search.gov.sg/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">website</a> for
more details.</p>

<p>With SearchSG, finding government information is faster, more accurate,
and more efficient than ever before.</p>
