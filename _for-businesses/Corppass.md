<h3>Bringing corporate entities easy access to government services with Corppass</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Products and Services/Corppass_screenshot.jpg">
</div>
<p>Corppass is the authorisation system for entities to manage digital service
access of employees who need to perform corporate transactions (Such as
grant applications and filing of corporate taxes) with Government agencies
in a safe and reliable manner</p>
<p>Corppass is meant for local businesses and organisations with a Unique
Entity Number (UEN) and foreign entities are entitled for Corppass</p>
<p>one of GovTech's efforts to streamline government-business transactions
(Source SmartNation)</p>
<p>(Source GovTech Website)</p>
<p>The service is currently used by more than 550,000 entities to conduct
online transactions for over 250 services with agencies and to manage digital
access of employees.</p>
<h3>Why use Corppass? </h3>
<p>Features of Corppass - access to more than 250 government digital services,
brings convenience by providing a single platform for corporate entities
to conduct online transactions, provides flexibility by allowing entities
to grant their employees separate roles when transacting with agencies</p>
<p>For business owners, manage employees via Corppass to transact on your
behalf</p>
<p>(Source SGDP Website)</p>
<h3>How to use Corppass?</h3>
<div class="iframe-wrapper">
<iframe style="max-width: 100%;" height="315" width="560" allowfullscreen="true" frameborder="0" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/0xcHKX_So2o?si=8rPChJmh2gWuEOMJ"></iframe>
</div>
<p>Determine if you are the Registered Officer (your name is officially registered
to your entity with ACRA) , Admin (appointed as Corppass Administrator
and will manage Corppass user accounts within your business), or User.</p>
<p>Prepare the following info if you are an Admin: Entity's UEN, your personal
information, registered officer's information:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Access Corppass website and log in with your Singpass to access Corppass</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create user accounts for other users within your entity</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select digital services which your entity transacts with</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assign digital services to users within your entity</p>
</li>
</ol>
