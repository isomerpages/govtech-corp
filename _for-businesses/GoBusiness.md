<h3>Simplifying business registration and administrative processes with GoBusiness</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Products and Services/News_GoBiz_Portal_banner.jpg">
</div>
<p>GoBusiness is an online platform to connect business owners to various
Government e-services and resources. This includes registering a business,
applying for licenses and grants, and more.</p>
<p>It serves as a single, reliable government touchpoint for businesses at
every stage of their journey - from starting, to running and growing the
business. This includes providing businesses in Singapore with integrated,
personalised and seamless access to business info and services from the
government.</p>
<p>In 2021, GoBusiness facilitated more than 6 million Government-to-Business
transactions, and more than 56,000 individuals and businesses benefited
from the e-Adviser recommendations.</p>
<h3>Why Use GoBusiness?</h3>

<p>Key features include user-friendly website with consolidated information,
step-by-step guidance for new businesses, personalised dashboard for simplified
transactions, 200+ e-Advisers to provide personalised recommendations on
various business topics</p>

</p>
<p>Businesses can easily access 120+ government e-services, find updated
regulatory resources, and keep business transactions in one place</p>
