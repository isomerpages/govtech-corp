<h2>Elevate Your Job Search with AI-Powered Tools</h2>
<p>In today's competitive job market, AI-powered tools are making it easier
for job seekers to find the right career opportunities and improve their
chances of landing job interviews. From personalised job recommendations
to resume optimisation, these GovTech products help match job skills to
vacancies and ensure that resumes are tailored for both recruiters and
applicant tracking systems, giving job seekers a significant advantage
in their job search journey.</p>
