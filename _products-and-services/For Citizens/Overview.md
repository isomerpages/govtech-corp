<h2>Our services for citizens</h2>
<p>Singapore's future will be one that's technology-enabled. As part of our
Digital Government Blueprint, GovTech has undertaken numerous ambitious
efforts to drive digitalisation on a wider scale.</p>
<p>One such aspect is expanding our suite of citizen services. Enabling the
widespread adoption of digital technologies among citizens is key to fast-tracking
Singapore's plans to become a Smart Nation. Our efforts have led to the
advent of powerful yet easy-to-use services such as Singpass, SGFinDex,
and GoWhere, all aimed at improving the lives of citizens.</p>
