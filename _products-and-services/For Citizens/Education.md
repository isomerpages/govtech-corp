<h2>Building a collaborative tech ecosystem between parents, teachers, and students</h2>
<p>As part of our efforts to support nation-wide digital transformation,
we support Singapore's education system by using tech to create a seamless
and connected educational experience for students, parents, and teachers.
These platforms strengthen school-home partnerships and foster a future-ready,
collaborative learning environment that benefits all stakeholders in the
education process.</p>
