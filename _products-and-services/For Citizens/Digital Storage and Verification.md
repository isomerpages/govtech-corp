<h3><strong>Creating Digital Storage and Verification services to secure the sharing of verified official documents</strong></h3>
<p>Digitalisation is a big part of our transition into a Smart Nation. As
communications go digital, a protocol is necessary in order to facilitate
the endorsement and transmission of crucial or sensitive documents between
the government and citizens.</p>
<p>The Digital Storage and Verification services serve this need by helping
us certify the validity of official documents while making sure we can
access and archive important government documents in one place.</p>
