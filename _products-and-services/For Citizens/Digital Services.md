<h3>Creating Digital Services to ensure government services are always accessible</h3>
<p>As part of our DGB goals, our vision is to create a government that is
"Digital to the core, and serves with Heart". With the goal of designing
for citizen's needs first, we aim to simplify transactions and e-payments
to ensure that citizen transactions with the government are easy, seamless
and secure.</p>
<p>To date, 99% of our government services are digital from end to end. Beyond
fulfilling e-government needs, these digital solutions create an open and
connected system for our private sector partners to tap into in order to
deliver services safely and readily.</p>
