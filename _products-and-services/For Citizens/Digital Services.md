<h3><strong>Creating Digital Services to ensure government services are always accessible</strong></h3>
<p>As part of our DGB goals, our vision is to create a government that is
"Digital to the core, and serves with Heart". With the goal of designing
for citizen's needs first, we aim to simplify transactions and e-payments
to ensure that citizen transactions with the government is easy, seamless
and secure. To date, 99% of our government services are digital from end-to-end.
Beyond fulfilling e-government needs, these digital solutions creates an
open and connected system for our private sector partners to tap on in
order to deliver services safely and readily.</p>
