<h3>Building a solid digital infrastructure as the foundation of a Digital Government</h3>
<p>As we move to digital, it is important to ensure stringent security measures
are in place and built into our infrastructure. This is especially to counter
possible issues like the loss of confidential or government data, the compromise
of protected citizen information, and more, as our public officers carry
out their work day to day. From activities like surfing the internet, and
sending emails to downloading files, our products under ICT and Infrastructure
ensure the safeguarding of all sensitive information, upholding the trust
that has been upon us as the Singapore Government.</p>
