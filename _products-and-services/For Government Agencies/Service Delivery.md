<h2>Enhancing public service delivery through seamless digital solutions</h2>
<p>Government agencies play a vital role in delivering services efficiently
to citizens. To support this, GovTech provides digital platforms that streamline
service operations, improve coordination, and enhance user experience.</p>
<p>GatherSG simplifies case management and inter-agency collaboration for
social and public service initiatives, while BookingSG offers a unified
appointment and resource management system across Whole-of-Government.
These solutions help agencies optimise workflows, automate processes, and
deliver seamless services to the public.</p>
