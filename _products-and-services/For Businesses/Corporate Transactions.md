<h3>Making business transactions and operations convenient and secure</h3>
<p>In support of the Smart Nation objectives, we aim to create secure and
reliable digital services not just for citizens, but also businesses in
Singapore.</p>
<p>We've launched solutions that make the experience of performing corporate
transactions a safe and seamless one. Now, businesses enjoy superb convenience
as they can do away with physical verification and manual processes such
as form filling. There is also a one-stop portal that offers information
on government support schemes and grants.</p>
