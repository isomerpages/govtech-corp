<h2>Parking convenience at your fingertips with <a href="http://Parking.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Parking.sg</a></h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Parking.sg" src="/images/Products and Services/For citizens/Parking_sg.png">
</div>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Parking.sg eliminates the need for parking coupons, offering drivers a
seamless, paperless parking experience.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Conveniently park, pay, extend sessions on-the-go and automatically receive
refunds on the app.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p>Say goodbye to parking coupons with <a href="https://www.parking.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Parking.sg</u></a>, a user-friendly mobile
app designed to make managing parking sessions effortless for drivers.
With an intuitive user interface, Parking.sg ensures a smooth, hassle-free
experience.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Drivers can pay for parking, receive timely reminders, and even extend
sessions remotely—all from their mobile devices. Any unused parking time
is automatically refunded through the app, giving you peace of mind and
eliminating the worry of overpaying for parking.&nbsp;</p>

<h3>Why use Parking.sg? &nbsp;</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Parking.sg app" src="/images/Products and Services/For citizens/Parking_sg_display.png">
</div>
<p>Parking.sg replaces paper parking coupons for a more convenient parking
experience. Here's how:&nbsp;</p>
<div class="isomer-card-grid">
<div class="isomer-card">
<div class="isomer-card-body">
<div class="isomer-card-title">Calculate fees automatically</div>
<div class="isomer-card-description">Upon entering your vehicle number, car park, and parking duration, charges
are calculated automatically and paid via card payment.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="isomer-card">
<div class="isomer-card-body">
<div class="isomer-card-title">Extend parking session remotely</div>
<div class="isomer-card-description">Parking.sg tracks your parking session and sends notifications before
expiry, enabling convenient extension of sessions remotely.</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="isomer-card">
<div class="isomer-card-body">
<div class="isomer-card-title">Get a refund</div>
<div class="isomer-card-description">Parking.sg calculates and refunds drivers on a per minute basis for parking
sessions that end earlier than expected.</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<h3>How to use Parking.sg? &nbsp;</h3>
<p>Start using Parking.sg in just five easy steps! &nbsp;</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Download the Parking.sg app.</p>
<p><a href="https://itunes.apple.com/vn/app/parking-sg/id1286602494?mt=8" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Download for iOS</u></a> 
<a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=sg.parking.streetsmart&amp;hl=en" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">&nbsp;<u>Download for Android</u>
</a>&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter your car park, vehicle number and parking duration.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Start your session and pay using debit/credit card.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Extend your session if needed.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Get a refund when you end you session early.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ol>
