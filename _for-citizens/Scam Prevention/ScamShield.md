<h2>Safeguarding citizens from scams with ScamShield</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="ScamShield Logo" src="/images/Products and Services/For citizens/ScamShield_Logo_600x400.png">

<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>ScamShield is a scam prevention tool that helps citizens check for scams,
block and filter scams, and report potential scams.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A suite of tools consisting of the 24/7 ScamShield helpline (reachable
at 1799), ScamShield app, a website, and social channels (WhatsApp and
Telegram).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Empowers citizens to protect themselves and others against scams across
various platforms.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Helped to blacklist and block over 120,000 scam entities since 2022.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p>Scams have been on the rise in recent years, with many citizens falling
victim to scammers and suffering hefty losses. In a bid to strengthen Singaporeâ€™s
scam prevention efforts, <a href="https://www.scamshield.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>ScamShield</u></a> was established
as a joint effort by the Ministry of Home Affairs, the Singapore Police
Force, the National Crime Prevention Council, and Open Government Products.</p>
<p>ScamShield consolidates all anti-scam resources onto a single, trusted
product suite that empowers citizens to combat scams. The suite consists
of the ScamShield helpline (reachable at 1799), ScamShield app (for iPhone
and Android), a website (<a href="https://www.scamshield.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>www.scamshield.gov.sg</u></a>)
with a range of anti-scam resources, and ScamShield Alert Social Channels
on WhatsApp and Telegram. Together, these tools form a robust safety net
that crucial in keeping the community safe from scams and fraudulent activities.</p>
<p>Since May 2022, ScamShield has blacklisted and blocked over 120,000 scam
entities and the app has had over 900,000 downloads since its launch.</p>
