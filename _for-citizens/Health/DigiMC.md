<h2>Digitising medical certificates for healthcare providers, patients, and employers with DigiMC&nbsp;</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="DigiMC" src="/images/Products and Services/For citizens/DigiMC.png">
</div>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Paperless DigiMC streamlines MC issuance and verification to enhance security,
prevent forgery, and increase accessibility for providers, patients, and
employers.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Digital MC can only be accessed via a unique link hosted under the mc.gov.sg
domain, cementing its authenticity and security.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Patients can receive digital MC recognisable as legitimate by employers,
on-the-go without waiting.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Trusted and used widely across 600+ GPs and 21 Public Healthcare Institutions.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p>In line with GovTech’s Smart Nation, <a href="https://site.mc.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>DigiMC</u></a> was introduced to replace
hardcopy MCs as the new norm, streamlining MC issuance and submission.
Digitisation of the MC issuance process creates a seamless, efficient,
and secure system for healthcare providers to issue, patients to receive,
and employers to verify medical leave.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>DigiMC eliminates the need for physical storage, allows medical records
to remain in-sync, and reduces time spent on administrative tasks with
patients. Patients no longer have to worry about misplacing paper MCs or
having to request for a replacement, enhancing the ease of submission to
their employer or school.&nbsp;</p>
<p>To date, DigiMC is trusted and used widely across 600+ GPs and 21 Public
Healthcare Institutions.&nbsp;</p>

the entire MC issuance process. Here's how:&nbsp;</p>
